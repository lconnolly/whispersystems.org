We build secure, easy-to-use communication tools to protect your privacy.

Founded in 2010 by security researcher Moxie Marlinspike and roboticist Stuart Anderson, Whisper Systems develops secure protocols that power encrypted voice calls and encrypted text messaging. In 2013, Whisper Systems became Open Whisper Systems, a community of dedicated developers and contributors working on the cutting edge of private, secure communication.

Open Whisper Systems receives funding from the Freedom of the Press Foundation, the Knight Foundation, the Shuttleworth Foundation and the Open Technology Fund, and is not owned by Twitter.

Our Technology

As the creators of forward and future secrecy, we work are working at the forefront of advanced cryptographic protocol development.

Our technologies have been recognized by the Electronic Frontier Foundation, whistleblower Edward Snowden, and internal NSA documents for providing secure, encrypted communications that protect your privacy.

Timeline

  1. 2010 Whisper Systems is founded by Moxie Marlinspike and Stuart Anderson to build secure encrypted communications protocols
  2. 2011 Twitter acquires Whisper Systems and releases its secure protocols as open source software
  3. 2012 Redphone and TextSecure are released as free, open source software
  4. 2013 Whisper Systems becomes Open Whisper Systems, a free, open source software community
  5. 2014 Signal is released to make encrypted phone calls on iOS
  6. 2014 Flock is released for private contact and calendar sync on Android
  7. 2014 WhatsApp integrates the TextSecure to providing end-to-end encryption for 600+ million users around the world
